#raw genetic data databases
1000 Genomes Project (IGSR) - internationalgenome.org (IGSR)
ClinPGx — formerly PharmGKB (pharmgkb.org)	Central pharmacogenomics knowledgebase for gene–drug relationships, variant annotations, clinical annotations, drug labels and guidelines.
ENCODE Portal (DCC) — encodeproject.org	Functional-genomics and epigenomics experiments, including transcription-factor binding, chromatin accessibility, histone modifications and regulatory annotations.
CIViC — civicdb.org	Open, community-curated interpretations of cancer variants, diagnoses, drugs and clinical evidence, including somatic variant–drug evidence statements and provenance.
Database of Genomic Variants (DGV) - dgv.tcag.ca
NCBI Gene	Gene-centred records linking sequences, expression, phenotypes, pathways, variation and publications.
OMIM — Online Mendelian Inheritance in Man	Authoritative, literature-curated descriptions of human genes, genetic phenotypes and Mendelian disorders.
GeneReviews	Expert-written, peer-reviewed chapters on inherited disorders, diagnosis, testing, management and counselling.
Orphanet	Rare-disease classifications, genes, epidemiology, diagnostic resources and expert centres.
GeneCards	Integrative human-gene knowledgebase combining genomic, transcriptomic, proteomic, disease and literature information from many sources.
DISGENET	Gene–disease and variant–disease associations extracted and curated from publications and other databases. Access conditions now include commercial plans.
ClinVar	Public archive of submitted clinical interpretations for variants associated with inherited disease, cancer phenotypes and drug response.
ClinGen	Expert assessment of gene–disease validity, dosage sensitivity and variant pathogenicity, plus clinical interpretation standards.
HGMD — Human Gene Mutation Database	Curated published germline variants associated with inherited disease. The current professional version is commercial; a delayed public version also exists.
dbSNP	NCBI archive of short genetic variants and their identifiers, including SNVs and small insertions/deletions.
dbVar	NCBI archive for human structural variants, including deletions, duplications, inversions, insertions and complex variants.
gnomAD	Population allele frequencies, constraint metrics and sequence variation from large aggregated human cohorts. Essential for identifying variants that are too common to explain rare disease.
LOVD — Leiden Open Variation Database	Gene-specific and patient-centred collections of sequence variants, often maintained by disease experts.
DECIPHER	Genotype–phenotype matching and sharing for rare disease, sequence variants, copy-number variants and patient phenotypes.
MaveDB	Repository for multiplexed assays of variant effect and deep-mutational-scanning results. Useful for functional evidence.
NHGRI-EBI GWAS Catalog	Curated genome-wide association studies, associations, traits and summary statistics.
PGS Catalog	Published polygenic scores with variant weights, development cohorts, ancestry information and performance evaluations.
PanelApp	Expert-reviewed diagnostic gene panels, including Genomics England and NHS-related panels.
OncoKB	Precision-oncology knowledgebase connecting tumour alterations with biological and therapeutic implications, targeted therapies, evidence levels and resistance associations.
VICC Meta-Knowledgebase	Aggregates and harmonises somatic cancer variant interpretations from multiple precision-oncology knowledgebases.
EWAS Catalog	Curated epigenome-wide association studies, particularly associations between DNA methylation and traits or diseases.
EWAS Atlas / EWAS Open Platform	Curated EWAS associations, traits, genes, methylation sites and biological annotations.
SCREEN	Search interface for ENCODE candidate cis-regulatory elements, including enhancers and promoters.
IHEC Data Portal	Reference epigenomes produced by the International Human Epigenome Consortium.
Roadmap Epigenomics	Human tissue and cell-type epigenomes. Roadmap data are now commonly accessed through ENCODE, GEO and associated portals.
GEO — Gene Expression Omnibus	Functional-genomics repository containing expression, methylation, ChIP-seq, chromatin accessibility, single-cell and other high-throughput experiments.
BioStudies / ArrayExpress collection	Functional-genomics studies previously accessed through ArrayExpress. The old standalone ArrayExpress interface was retired and its collection moved into BioStudies.
MethBank	DNA-methylation profiles, methylomes and methylation annotations across organisms and biological conditions.
DiseaseMeth	Disease-associated human DNA-methylation alterations collected from published studies.
EpiFactors	Epigenetic regulators, chromatin-modifying enzymes, complexes, targets and products.
ChIP-Atlas	Uniformly processed public ChIP-seq and related epigenomic datasets, with enrichment and target-analysis tools.
PubMed/MEDLINE	Primary biomedical literature search for genetics, molecular biology, medicine, nutrition and pharmacogenomics. Contains citations and abstracts with links to publishers or repositories.
PubMed Central — PMC	Free full-text archive of biomedical and life-sciences articles.
Europe PMC	Publications, abstracts, full text, preprints and related biological data. Particularly useful for text mining and API access.
Embase	Subscription biomedical and pharmacological index. Especially valuable for drug safety, adverse reactions, clinical studies and systematic reviews.
Scopus	Broad abstract and citation database; useful for citation tracking and discovering related papers.
Web of Science	Multidisciplinary citation database for forward/backward citation searching and systematic reviews.
Dimensions	Links publications with grants, datasets, clinical trials, patents and organisations.
OpenAlex	Open scholarly catalogue covering papers, authors, institutions, concepts and citations; useful for bulk data and APIs.
Semantic Scholar	AI-assisted article discovery, citation networks and related-paper recommendations.
Google Scholar	Very broad discovery tool covering journal articles, theses, repositories, books and conference papers. Coverage is wide but metadata can be messy.
Crossref Metadata Search	DOI and publication-metadata search across articles, books, datasets and other scholarly works.
DOAJ	Directory and search platform for peer-reviewed open-access journals and articles.
CORE	Aggregates open-access papers from institutional and subject repositories.
OpenAIRE Explore	Searches publications, datasets, software, projects and repositories in the European and international open-science ecosystem.
BASE	Academic search engine aggregating records from institutional repositories and other scholarly sources.
bioRxiv	Biology preprints, including genetics, genomics, epigenetics and bioinformatics. Not peer-reviewed by default.
medRxiv	Health-science and clinical preprints. Results should not be treated as established clinical evidence before peer review.
Cochrane Library	Systematic reviews and controlled-trial evidence, including some pharmacogenetic and nutritional interventions.
AGRICOLA	USDA literature index covering agriculture, food, nutrition, plant and animal genetics.
CAB Abstracts	Subscription database covering applied life sciences, nutrition, agriculture, food, veterinary medicine and genetics.
FSTA — Food Science and Technology Abstracts	Specialist literature database for food science, nutrition, food composition, metabolism and related health research.
LitVar2	Searches biomedical literature by genetic variant, gene, disease or chemical, including pharmacogenetic and drug-response variants and their synonyms.
Mastermind Genomic Intelligence	Commercial/freemium genomic-literature search engine indexing full-text variant mentions, including rare variant–drug and variant–phenotype combinations.
VarSome	Variant aggregator combining numerous annotation and clinical databases, ACMG evidence and community interpretations.
Franklin by Genoox	Automated variant annotation, prioritisation and ACMG-oriented interpretation platform.
Ensembl	Genome annotation, genes, transcripts, comparative genomics, regulatory elements and variation across many species.
UCSC Genome Browser	Visualises genomic coordinates and annotation tracks; includes tools for variant annotation, sequence alignment and coordinate conversion.
NutriGenomeDB	Nutrigenomics experiments connecting dietary interventions or food compounds with gene-expression responses.
dbNP — Nutritional Phenotype Database	Nutrition-study metadata, phenotypes, interventions and omics measurements.
NutriChem	Literature-derived relationships among foods, phytochemicals, diseases and drugs. It is an older resource, so availability and update status should be checked before building software around it.
Exposome-Explorer	Biomarkers of dietary and environmental exposures extracted from biomarker and epidemiological studies.
Phenol-Explorer	Food polyphenol content, metabolism, pharmacokinetics and intervention-study information.
FooDB	Food constituents, chemical structures, concentrations, biological effects and associated references.
PhytoHub	Dietary phytochemicals and their human metabolites, including chemical and literature information.
CPIC — Clinical Pharmacogenetics Implementation Consortium	Peer-reviewed guidelines explaining how available genetic results should be used when selecting or dosing medications.
PharmVar	Standardised pharmacogene haplotypes and star-allele definitions, such as CYP2D6, CYP2C19 and TPMT alleles.
PharmCAT	Software that converts genetic variant data into pharmacogene diplotypes, phenotypes and guideline-based reports.
FDA Table of Pharmacogenetic Associations	FDA-reviewed gene–drug relationships for therapeutic management, efficacy or adverse-event risk.
FDA Table of Pharmacogenomic Biomarkers in Drug Labeling	Drugs whose FDA labels mention germline or somatic pharmacogenomic biomarkers.
DGIdb — Drug–Gene Interaction Database	Aggregates known and potentially druggable gene–drug interactions from publications and source databases. It is more suitable for hypothesis generation than direct prescribing.
